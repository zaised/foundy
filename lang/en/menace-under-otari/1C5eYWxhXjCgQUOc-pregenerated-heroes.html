Other Characters

If you want to expand the choices at your table, any of the level 1 heroes from the Iconics compendium would serve well. However, some of them may feature more complicated classes or features than those included in the Beginner Box.

In addition, you can always make your own heroes from scratch. In that case, consult the @JournalEntry[KIpNnMtah73eyPC8]{Character Creation} journal entry.
