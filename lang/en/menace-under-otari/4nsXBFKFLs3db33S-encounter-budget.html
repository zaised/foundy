Table 10-2: Creature XP and Role

Choose a number of creatures whose XP total adds up to your budget. Remember to use a good mix of levels! Creatures that are one or two levels above your players make for good bosses but can be frustrating to fight often. Creatures three or more levels above them are extremely deadly threats even by themselves!
