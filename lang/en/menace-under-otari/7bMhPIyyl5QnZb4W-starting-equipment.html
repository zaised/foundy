The kinds of equipment you can use is determined by your proficiencies and training. Usually, these will come from your class. For instance, a fighter is Trained in all weapons, but a wizard is limited to a much smaller selection.

Starting Equipment in the Core Rules

In the Core Rules, your character starts at first level with 15 gold pieces to spend on basic equipment. If you know which items you'd like to buy or would like to browse, you can open the Compendium Browser at the bottom of the Compendiums tab of your sidebar and search for them there, or consult a copy of the Pathfinder Core Rulebook.

Class Kits

If you want to start quickly, we suggest you choose to purchase one of the following "class kits", which will quickly give you a broadly useful set of equipment in exchange for a set amount of gold. To do so, simply drag it from the table to your character sheet.

Some of these class kits also include additional options, which suggest some of the ways you could spend your leftover gold. For instance, the Wizard class kit costs 3gp, but you could optionally pay an additional 3gp and 2 sp for a crossbow and 20 bolts.
