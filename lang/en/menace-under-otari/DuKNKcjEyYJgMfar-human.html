As unpredictable and varied as any of Golarion's peoples, humans have exceptional drive and the capacity to endure and expand. Though many civilizations thrived before humanity rose to prominence, humans have built some of the greatest and most abhorrent societies throughout the course of history. Today they are the most populous people in realms around the world.

Playing a Human

Choose the @Compendium[pf2e.ancestries.IiG7DgeLWYrSNXuX]{Human} ancestry by dragging it onto your character sheet. This will automatically apply several of the features associated with the Human ancestry to your character, which are explained here.

Hit Points: Humans are not as sturdy as dwarves, nor as frail as elves. You have 8 hit points from your ancestry..

Speed: Humans move about as quickly as most ancestries. You have a speed of 25 from your ancestry.

Names

Unlike many ancestral cultures, which generally cleave to specific traditions and shared histories, humanity's diversity has resulted in a near-infinite set of names. Humans' names are as varied as their beliefs and appearances.

Human Heritages

Like for most folk in the world, there are many different kinds of humans.

Each heritage grants benefits which reflect your character's past, education, and experiences. If you need some help, we suggest you pick one of the following heritages.
